Device Trees on the BeagleBone



Available device trees: (precompiled)
	/lib/firmware
	
	
To view loaded device trees and their slots:
	cat /sys/devices/bone_capemgr*/slots

To (temporarily) load a device tree:
	echo (device tree name, no 00A0 or extension) >/sys/devices/bone_capemgr*/slots

To (temporarily) unload a device tree:
	echo -(slot number) >/sys/devices/bone_capemgr*/slots

To load/disable a device tree at boot: Edit		/boot/uboot/uEnv.txt
	cape_enable=capemgr.enable_partno= (device tree name, no 00A0 or extension)
	cape_disable=capemgr.disable_partno= (device tree name, no 00A0 or extension)

	
	
To decompile device trees into source:
	dtc -I dtb -O dts (input file) > (outputfile)

To compile device trees:
	dtc -O dtb -o (output file) -b 0 -@ (input file)
